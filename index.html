AngelList Policy Center

Policy for Modern Private Markets: Responsible Access, Smart Liquidity, Secure Identity

We advocate for regulatory reforms that:

•
Expand access to private markets through professionally managed funds, where investors benefit from fiduciary obligations, diversification, and institutional diligence
•
Empower investors by raising 3(c)(1) thresholds, enabling GPs to raise capital from larger, more diverse, and distributed investors.
•
Investor Passports that allow investors to verify regulatory and compliance status without compromising privacy

Our goal: Accelerate the rate of innovation by modernizing rules to increase private market participation without compromising investor protections.
