Modernize Finance Policy: Responsible Access, Smart Liquidity, Secure Identity

We advocate for regulatory reforms that:

•
Expand access to private markets through professionally managed funds—with safeguards intact
•
Improve liquidity when startups stay private longer, so early investors can fuel the next generation of innovation
•
Investor Passports that allow investors to verify regulatory and compliance status without compromising privacy

The goal: Broaden opportunity responsibly by aligning rules with today's markets—protecting investors while unlocking growth.
